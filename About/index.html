Zo DiSanto (born 1999, United States of America) is an undergraduate student at Pace University, studying Computer Science.

Zo has experience as a Teaching Assistant (TA) for CSC 120 / ISC 150 Intro into Programming and Programming Languages for two semesters (Fall 2019, Spring 2020), and ISC 250 Programming Through Mobile App Development for one semester (Spring 2020).
In August 2018 through May 2019, Zo was a Resident Asistant (RA). Here, is where he improved his skills on project/program planning, organizing and implementation, how to work in a team, and how to advise students with different backgrounds, majors and ages.
