Brownie Recipe

Recipe Data

Yield: 16 servings

Prep: 10 minutes

Cook: 30 minutes

Ready: 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter

  • 2 ounces unsweetened chocolate

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 2 large eggs

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF

  2. In medium saucepan, melt butter and chocolate

  3. Remove from heat and stir in sugar

  4. Carefully fold in eggs one at a time

  5. Add vanilla, stir in flour and nuts, and mix well

  6. Spread mixture into greased 8x8x2" pan

  7. Bake at 350ºF for 30 minutes (avoid overbaking!)

  8. Let cool completely

  9. Cut into 16 squares and serve
