John D. Murray Firefighters Museum

Oswego, NY

The John D. Murray Firefighters Museum is a museum that houses historical fire engines, firemen and memorabilia. Firefighting in Oswego began in 1895, so there is a large amount of information that can be uncovered. Focusing on how the firefighting community has impacted the Oswego community and the relationship between the two as they have been working together for 100+ years. This website will have everything from change in location, community reputation, technology changes and even firefighting training.


This webpage was created by SUNY Oswego Hypermedia Class Project in Spring 2020
