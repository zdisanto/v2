Welcome!

At PetSearch we help pets find a foreverhome. There have been a lot of complaints from animal lovers in Elmira, NY. Many saying that it is difficult for them to search and find the pets through an online website platform. This problem even became news headlines in Central New York! With that in mind, few students from SUNY Oswego who were taking a database management class tried to resolve that issue by introducing a website that primarily serves as a search engine for pet lovers. The website is potentially designed to meet the needs of local Central New Yorkers who like to browse the website and find their forever friend.
