Welcome!

My name is Edward A. Sheldon. I was born October 4, 1823 in Perry Center New York and passed August 26, 1897 in Oswego New York. I went to and graduated from Hamilton College. I was the 1st President of State University of New York (SUNY) at Oswego. When I founded it, it was called Oswego Primary Teachers' Training School. I got married to Frances Stiles Sheldon and we had one child. A little girl named Mary. She too became an educator.
