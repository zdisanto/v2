Brief:

This website was created for the second assignment in ISC (Information Science) 405, taught by David Vampola at SUNY Oswego, created by Zo DiSanto. The aim was to take a historical figure and have their journal entries turned into a blog. The information is not my own and taken directly from Edward A. Sheldon's Autobiography cited below. The website is left unfinished to prove creating something like this is tangeble and with more time can be completed with a proper time frame.
